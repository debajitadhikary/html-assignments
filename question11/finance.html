Finance Course

Course Description: The Finance course covers key concepts in financial management, investment analysis, and corporate finance. Students will gain insights into financial markets, risk management, and financial planning, equipping them for careers in banking, investment, and financial consulting.

Instructors

  • Dr. John Maynard Keynes - Professor of Finance
  • Dr. Benjamin Graham - Associate Professor of Investment Analysis
  • Ms. Janet Yellen - Lecturer in Corporate Finance
