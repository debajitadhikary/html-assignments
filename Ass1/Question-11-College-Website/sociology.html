Sociology Course

Course Description: The Sociology course provides an in-depth understanding of societal structures, social behavior, and cultural norms. Students will explore various sociological theories and methods to analyze social phenomena and their impacts on communities.

  • Dr. Michael Green - Associate Professor of Social Research
  • Ms. Lisa White - Lecturer in Social Policy
