Economics Course

Course Description: The Economics course provides a comprehensive study of economic principles, theories, and applications. Students will explore topics such as microeconomics, macroeconomics, and econometrics, preparing them for careers in economic analysis, policy-making, and business strategy.

Instructors

  • Dr. Adam Smith - Professor of Economics
  • Dr. Milton Friedman - Associate Professor of Economic Theory
  • Ms. Elinor Ostrom - Lecturer in Public Economics
