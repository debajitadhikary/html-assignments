Mathematics Course

Course Description: The Mathematics course delves into various mathematical concepts and techniques, including algebra, calculus, and statistics. Students will develop a strong foundation in mathematical theories and problem-solving skills, preparing them for careers in engineering, data science, and academic research.

Instructors

  • Dr. Carl Friedrich Gauss - Associate Professor of Algebra
  • Ms. Mary Cartwright - Lecturer in Calculus
