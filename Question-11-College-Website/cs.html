Computer Science Course

Course Description: The Computer Science course offers an in-depth study of computer systems, programming, algorithms, and software development. Students will gain hands-on experience in various programming languages and technologies, preparing them for careers in software engineering, data analysis, and more.

Instructors

  • Dr. Alan Turing - Professor of Computer Science
  • Dr. Grace Hopper - Associate Professor of Software Engineering
  • Ms. Ada Lovelace - Lecturer in Algorithms
